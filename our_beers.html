Oreo Stout - 6.0%

This stout was made using Magnum and East Kent Golding hops. The beer was aged on two packages of Double Stuffed Oreos to bring in that Oreo flavor we were looking for.

Pale Ale - 4.1%

This experimental Pale Ale was made using Galaxy and Mosaic hops. Additional Galaxy and Mosaic dry hops were added to the secondary fermenter.

Blueberry IPA - 8.0%

A Milkshake IPA made with two pounds of lactose and half gallon of blueberry puree.

Oktoberfest - 4.6%

This lager was made using Spalter and Hallertauer hops and then lagered for two months.
